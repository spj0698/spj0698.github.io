A Little Bit About Myself

Hi, I'm Sagar Joshi and I'm a junior student majoring in Computer Science at California State University, Fullerton.

My goal in life is to be a part of a team that constructs beneficial, efficient and convenient software for consumers. I want to continue innovating and improving software that could benefit others by assisting them with certain tasks and making the process easier for them. I chose to major in computer science because it offers great opportunities for true creativity and innovativeness while also getting a deeper look at the theories behind the technology we use every day. As a student, I want to understand and develop a structured way of thinking and a proven approach to problem-solving, while also being able to understand the collaborative side of computer science with other fields.

Currently, I am serving as the university's Engineering Ambassador and working as a Undergraduate Research Assistant alongside Dr. Anand Panangadan to Understand Sustainability Behaviors from Text Data.
